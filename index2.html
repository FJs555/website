Social Bias in Algorithms

Due to the human element of programing. Algorithms/Artifical Intelligence have the potential to suffer from the same biases that humans have. They can end up discrimiating by race, gender, or any other social standards. The Ai can do this by unintinally filtering out keywords in resumes or other document, but there are other methods the algortihm can unintentially can gain bias

Examples of AI Bias

  • Feeding the algorthim real world data but data tends to favor men, so program filters out any application with the word Women
  • COMPAS(Correctional Offender Managment Profialing for Alernative Sanctions) The algorithm used over all doubled the amount of percieved recidivism for black offenders over white offenders.
  • An algorthim prediciting the needs of American health care and who would need extra heavily favored white patients over black patients because the algorthim used healthcare cost and for various reasons black patients incurred lower healthcare cost. Thus, Skewing the system

Some Solutions and where to look

  • Auditing Algothrims
  • Test Algothrims before and after uses
  • Increase Standards and Regulations of models used
  • Use mock data to gain a broader array of results
  • Spread the word on this topic
  • Join organizations such as the Algorithm Justice Leauge
